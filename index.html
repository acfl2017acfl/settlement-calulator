Quickly compare 3+1, 4+1 and 6+1 offers — installments always rounded up to the next ₹10.

Outstanding (₹) Offer Installment (₹) Total Collection (₹) Discount (₹)

Formula: Installment = ceil(Outstanding ÷ (PaidParts + 1) to next ₹10). Total Collection = Installment × PaidParts. Discount = Installment.

Rounding rule
Installment is always rounded up to the next multiple of ₹10 (e.g., ₹1,234 → ₹1,240).
